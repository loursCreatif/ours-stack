# Example prompts

## Darwin × évolution

**Trigger:** `/dialogue discuter de l'évolution avec Darwin` (alias `/dialogue-historique`)

**Wedge (ad-hoc):** comment la sélection naturelle explique une adaptation précise — pas "toute la biologie".

**Opening question (informed):** "You speak of 'evolution' as if it were one mechanism — which *observable* change in a lineage would convince you that natural selection, rather than mere variation, did the work?"

**Belief to challenge:** "evolution aims at progress" → Darwin stresses undirected variation.

## Tesla × électricité

**Trigger:** `/dialogue électricité avec Tesla`

**Wedge:** comprendre AC vs DC tradeoffs for power distribution — not full biography.

**Opening question:** "You wish to discuss electricity — are you troubled by the *force* itself, or by how men commercialize what nature offers freely?"

## Study-linked

**Trigger:** `/dialogue robotique-assemblage-structurel`

**Reads:** `studies/robotique-assemblage-structurel/brief.md`

**Suggested figures:** Brunelleschi (construction craft), Babbage (mechanism + division of labor), contemporary: Ishiguro (if user wants living figure — see living-figures rule in SKILL.md).

**Belief to challenge:** "bio-inspiration beats industrial arms for structural assembly."
